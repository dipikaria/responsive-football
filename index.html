Copa America 2021

The 2021 Copa America was the 47th edition of the Copa America, the match featured two rival teams: Argentina and the tournament hosts, Brazil. Argentina won the match 1–0 to clinch their fifteenth Copa America title and first since 1993, giving captain Lionel Messi his first senior international trophy sixteen years into his career.
